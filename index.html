What is Do a Barrel Roll

Do a Barrel Roll is a fun Google Easter egg that makes your search page spin 360 degrees. Whenever a user types do a barrel roll into the Google search bar and hits enter, the entire search page performs a full 360-degree spin. This fun activity has captured the attention of millions of users.

Where Did “Do a Barrel Roll” Come From?

The phrase "do a barrel roll" comes from the 1997 video game Star Fox 64 on the Nintendo 64 console. In the game, a character named Peppy Hare famously shouts the command, encouraging players to perform an aerial spinning move to dodge attacks. Since then, the phrase has become an iconic piece of gaming culture.

Google embraced the phrase by embedding a hidden trick that mimics the spinning action, bringing a piece of gaming nostalgia to the web. All you have to do is go to Google and search for “do a barrel roll,” and you’ll see the page rotate once.
